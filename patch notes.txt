Features:
- ALL nations except remnants get a unique flag, plus new flags for existing ones. (Special thanks to @Grand Marshal Elison)
- One provinced nations has special government names for flavour.
- Controlling Lich King's Crown grants you permanent Magical Enslavement CB against all undead.
- New Governments: Council Monarchy, Magic Kingdom, Opportunism, Hereditary Kingdom, Meritocracy, Magocracy. (Detailed info: https://wwu.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_All_Government_Reforms)
- An option to not become leader of the Horde for Rise of The Horde era orc players.
- Recruit and Building decisions for landless mode. (Note: They are playable through Nation Designer -> Landless Mode Reforms)
- WoW Guild Patterns for Nation Designer.
- Warchiefship succession system.
- Broken Isles Trade Node 
Improvements:
- Governmental Overhaul: Many government reforms had their names/stats/properties/abilities changed. (Detailed info: https://wwu.fandom.com/wiki/List_of_All_Government_Reforms)
- Workers Estate is now Alliance Leadership only.
- Economic and Points Menu overhaul. (Common change with ESU/GE)
- Horde Leadership now allows war between vassals (instead of alliances) and use new Warchief mechanic based on Mamluk.
- Entirely removed mass-migration mechanic due mass-confusion.
- Removed building construction events due their overpowered nature.
- Claim/Loot province decisions are now usable by tribes instead of savages & only visible during wartime.
- All admiralties now can use rulership system similar to Kul Tiras.
- New localisation and icon for missions.
Bugs:
- Fix: Double Legitimacy type when changing into Theocracy from another Government type.
- Fix: Dark Portal is not opening historically and dynamically pre-TBC era.
- Fix: Several errors in The Missing Diplomat event chain.
- Fix: Several position data fixes.
