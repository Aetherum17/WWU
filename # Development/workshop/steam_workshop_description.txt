[h1]Description:[/h1]
World of Warcraft Universalis is an ambitious project that aims to place the Warcraft universe within Europa Universalis IV. The mod will provide the flavour of Warcraft seemlessly integrated into the mechanics of Europa Universalis IV.

[h1]Features:[/h1]
[LIST]
[*] A completely custom map, with thousands of provinces, sufficiently modelling the world of Warcraft.
[*] Hundreds of countries to play, each with unique culturs, religions and gameplay mechanics.
[*] Tons of events, decisions and missions, providing vast amounts of flavour.
[*] Numerous unique mechanics, such as unique Faction mechanics that emulate the Alliance, Horde, and more.
[*] Bookmarks ranging from the Rise of the Horde on Draenor, up to the Mists of Pandaria.
[*] Hundreds of unique models, brining the visuals of Warcraft into Europa Universalis IV.
[*] Plenty of visual flavour that combines the visual motifs of Warcraft with those of Europa Universalis IV.
[/LIST]
