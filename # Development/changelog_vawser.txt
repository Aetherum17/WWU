#--------------------------------------
# Changelog
#--------------------------------------

# Events
- Increased the MTTH for wwu_gilneas.5 to 120 months (was 90 days)
- Changed wwu_scourge.35 event (Undead Plague in [Root.GetName]). 
 - Added two new options, one to spend some ADM power to weaken the rebel spawn, and another to spend MIL power to avoid the rebel spawn. 
 - Once 10 wwu_scourge.35 events have occurred, the Undead Plague stops.
 
# Factions
- When a faction leader assignment occurs, it will assign leadership to the player if they are not considered 'weak'. If they are considered weak, the assignment is random.
- Added a scaling modifier based on faction renown (25, 50, 75, 100) for each faction.

# Interface
- Added a visual indicator of the Ring of Peace spell usage in the diplomacy view, allowing the player to more easily notice if a country has war declaration blocked by the spell.
- Added a visual indicator of the Civilization process in the diplomacy view, allowing the player to notice if a country is transitioning from Uncivilized to Civilized.
- Added a visual icon for Civilized and Uncivilized countries in the diplomacy view.
- Added more information on the ruler's class and spellcaster stats/mana when hovering over the class/spellcaster icons in the diplomacy view.
- Added a Scry button in the province view. This allows the player to open an event that lets them focus the connected provinces that are part of a non-visible canal if applicable.
